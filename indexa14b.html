Adria Motors

Adria Motors is your local NYC German Auto Repair Specialist located in Long Island City NY specializing in Audi, BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Porches and Volkswagon automobiles. We utilize state of the art equipment.

Owner, Mille opened Adria Motors in 1974 and has been serving tri-State area costumers ever since. Numerous original customers, and their children, carry on bringing their cars to us because they value the personal interest we offer over unfriendly dealership service.
